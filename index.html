2D Frame by Frame Animation Series, 2020.
Collaborating with Hikmawanmie Studio, as Video Editor in the production process.

An animated series with a total of 5 episodes that tells the story of Jojo's life as the main character with the role of a child who has expressive characters and always thinks logically.

Lifting the light comedy genre with a conclusion at the end of each episode, all episodes in this animation discuss the main issue regarding the dangers of smoking, especially for children and families. As well as the reason behind the creation of this animation is as an auxiliary media for learning to children.

Behind My Suffering Life

2D Motion Graphic
1920 x 1080 / H.264 (.mp4) / 25 fps

Collaboration with Andalan Kami Production, as Director and Video Editor.

It is a short animated film that raises social issues regarding cases of bullying in the school area. Taking from the victim's point of view, this animation is expected to provide an overview of the mental and psychological condition of the victim from bullying, so that it is hoped that it can prevent other victims from occurring.


This work was included in several animation competitions, but only as a participant, some of these competitions were: 20th DigiCon6 ASIA Awards Indonesia Region 2018, and 12th Edition Hellofest Animation & Short Movie Festival 2018 in the Short Movie category.

Behind My Suffering Life

2D Motion Graphic
1920 x 1080 / H.264 (.mp4) / 25 fps

Bersama dengan AndalanKami Production, sebagai Director dan Video Editor.

Merupakan karya Film Animasi Pendek yang mengangkat tentang isu sosial mengenai kasus bullying (perundungan) di kawasan sekolah. Karya ini sempat diikutsertakan pada beberapa kompetisi animasi, namun hanya berstatus sebagai peserta, beberapa kompetisi tersebut adalah: 20th DigiCon6 ASIA Awards Wilayah Indonesia Tahun 2018, dan 12th Edition Hellofest Animation & Short Movie Festival Tahun 2018 pada kategori Short Movie.
